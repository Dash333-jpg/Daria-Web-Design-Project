Lebanon, officially known as the Lebanese Republic, is a small yet historically rich country located in the Levant region of Western Asia.Lebanon covers an area of approximately 10,452 square kilometers (4,036 sq mi) and has a population of over five million people. The capital and largest city is Beirut, known for its vibrant culture and history. The country features a diverse landscape, including the Lebanon Mountain range and a beautiful Mediterranean coastline, making it a unique destination.
